It's Easy

Go Camp makes reserving your next campsite a cool breeze. Simply start with the date you want to go, how far you are willing to drive and your zip code. We will search all available campsites in that radius. Click on the campsite you like and book directly with Reserve America or Recreation.gov.

Protecting Wilderness

We are hikers and love the outdoors. Every time you book a campsite from Go Camp we make a small fee. We donate 10% of our profits every year to groups protecting the wilderness. Let's keep California wild...go camp this weekend!
